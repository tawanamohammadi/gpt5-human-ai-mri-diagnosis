## ðŸ“Œ Project Overview

This repository contains the data, code, and resources for the research project investigating the role of large language models, specifically ChatGPT-5, in the interpretation of lumbar spine MRI scans. The study follows a multidisciplinary case study approach, documenting the entire research lifecycle from preprint to major revision.

Our goal is to demonstrate a reproducible **Human-AI diagnostic workflow** built upon a foundation of transparent and ethical AI frameworks.
